Max Siegel

Research Scientist  ·  MIT Brain & Cognitive Sciences

I study how humans understand the physical and social world — using computational modeling, behavioral experiments, and the lens of Bayesian inference. I'm a member of the Computational Cognitive Science group at MIT.
